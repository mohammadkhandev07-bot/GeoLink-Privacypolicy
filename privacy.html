GeoLink ("GeoLink," "we," "us," or "our") is a social media platform accessible via web browser and as a Progressive Web App (PWA), operated by an independent developer based in India. This Privacy Policy applies to the GeoLink website at https://geo-link-one.vercel.app, and to any related distributions of GeoLink through the Google Play Store, the Amazon Appstore, or any other platform that links to this policy (collectively, the "Service").

By creating an account or otherwise using GeoLink, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described here, please do not use the Service.

01Introduction

GeoLink is a social media platform that allows registered users to create profiles, publish posts containing text, photos, and videos, interact with other users' content through likes, comments, and shares, follow and be followed by other users, search for and view public profiles, and receive notifications relating to platform activity.

This Privacy Policy is designed to give you a clear, accurate, and complete understanding of GeoLink's data practices, in line with applicable data protection laws, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act"), and, where applicable to users located in those regions, the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") and the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), as well as the developer policies of Google Play and the Amazon Appstore.

This document is intended to be an original policy drafted specifically for GeoLink and its feature set, and should not be construed as a substitute for independent legal advice tailored to your specific jurisdiction or business circumstances.

03Why We Collect Information

We collect the categories of information described above for the following purposes:

  • To create and authenticate your GeoLink account and maintain your login session.
  • To enable core social features, including posting, commenting, liking, sharing, following, and searching for other users.
  • To personalize your experience, such as displaying relevant content and suggested profiles.
  • To send you notifications about activity relevant to your account (for example, new followers, comments, or likes).
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and stability of the Service, including detecting fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • To diagnose technical issues, prevent service disruptions, and improve performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To analyze aggregated usage trends in order to improve GeoLink's features and design.

04How We Use Your Information

GeoLink uses the information collected as follows:

4.1 Provision of the Service

Your account details, profile information, and content are used to operate the core functionality of GeoLink, including displaying your profile to other users, publishing your posts, and enabling interactions such as comments and likes.

4.2 Communication

We may use your email address to send account-related communications, including verification emails, password reset instructions, security alerts, and, where you have opted in, product updates.

4.3 Safety and Moderation

We may review reported content, account activity, device, and log information to investigate violations of our Terms of Service, prevent impersonation, and protect users from harassment, spam, or abusive behavior.

4.4 Analytics and Improvement

Where analytics tools are used, aggregated and, where possible, de-identified usage data helps us understand how features are used so that we can improve navigation, performance, and design.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

05Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable data protection law requires a legal basis for processing personal data, GeoLink relies on the following bases:

  • Consent — for example, when you voluntarily create an account, upload content, or agree to receive optional communications.
  • Performance of a contract — processing necessary to provide the Service you have signed up for, such as maintaining your profile and enabling social features.
  • Legitimate interests — such as securing the platform, preventing fraud, and improving the Service, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where processing is necessary to comply with applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process.

Users located in India are additionally protected under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, which governs the processing of digital personal data by data fiduciaries such as GeoLink.

06How Information Is Shared

GeoLink is a social platform, and certain information is inherently shared as part of using the Service:

  • Public profile information — your username, profile picture, bio, and public posts are visible to other users and, unless you restrict visibility through available settings, may be visible to the public.
  • Social interactions — your comments, likes, shares, and follower/following relationships are visible to other users in accordance with the features of the platform.
  • Service providers — we may share information with trusted infrastructure and hosting providers (for example, cloud hosting, storage, and analytics providers) strictly to operate and maintain the Service, under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal and safety disclosures — we may disclose information where required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or where necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of GeoLink, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — if GeoLink is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy or a policy offering equivalent protections.

We do not share your private account credentials or password with any third party.

09Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account remains active and for a reasonable period thereafter, as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service and maintain accurate records of your account and content.
  • Comply with applicable legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory requirements.
  • Resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and prevent fraud or abuse.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or the resolution of disputes. Content you have shared with other users (for example, comments left on another user's post) may remain visible if independently retained by that user's device or feed, consistent with the nature of social media publishing.

10Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using industry-standard protocols (such as HTTPS/TLS).
  • Secure storage of passwords using one-way cryptographic hashing.
  • Access controls limiting who may access user data internally.
  • Regular review of security practices as the platform evolves.
Important: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security, and you share information with us at your own risk.

12Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information via your profile settings or by contacting us.
  • Right to deletion/erasure — request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Right to restrict or object to processing — request that we limit certain uses of your information.
  • Right to data portability — request your information in a structured, commonly used format, where applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 17. We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

13Account & Data Deletion

You may delete your GeoLink account at any time through the account settings menu within the app, where available, or by emailing our support address with a verified deletion request from your registered email address.

Upon a valid deletion request, we will:

  • Deactivate your public profile and remove it from search and public view.
  • Delete or irreversibly anonymize your personal information within our systems within a reasonable period, subject to legal retention obligations described in Section 9.
  • Remove your posts, photos, and videos from active platform display, except copies that may remain in routine backups for a limited period before being purged.

Please note that content you posted which was reshared, commented on, or saved externally by other users prior to deletion may not be fully retrievable once shared, consistent with the nature of publicly posted social media content.
